## 🔬 What is Unified Axioconscious Field Theory (UAFT)?

UAFT is a foundational framework treating consciousness not as a property that emerges from matter, but as an atemporal ground from which both physical and experiential differentiation arise. The framework integrates work in philosophy of mind, theoretical psychology, quantum biology, foundations of physics, and consciousness studies to address questions that conventional approaches have left unresolved.

**Key concepts of UAFT include:**

* **Axioconsciousness** – The atemporal, self-grounding ground of all differentiation
* **Differentiation** – The mechanism through which axioconsciousness produces structure, time, and experience
* **Codifferential** – The non-dual relationship between gravity and time as paired expressions of one process
* **Colinear Conservation** – Noether's theorem reframed as the trace of active correction by colinear gravitytime
* **Pinch Point** – The structural threshold at which emotional saturation produces an integrated self
* **Emotional Tree and Emotional Spectrum** – The divergent and convergent structures of emotional life, joined by Emotional Compression as the active mechanism producing density across development
* **Binary Severance Threshold (BST)** – Structural account of which substrates can support consciousness
* **Hard Conflation, Concept Hollowing, and the Stolen Concept** – Three named fallacies driving the pragmatic pivot in contemporary AI consciousness research
* **Conscious Bias Taxonomy** – Twelve recurring patterns of inherited assumption that produce framework-bound paradoxes

## 📚 Published Research

All papers are open access on Zenodo with permanent DOIs and mirrored on PhilPapers.

* **Three Fallacies in AI Consciousness Research: A Structural Diagnostic** *(May 2026)* – Names three structural operations driving the pragmatic pivot in contemporary AI consciousness research: Hard Conflation, Concept Hollowing, and the Stolen Concept. Comșa (2026) is the worked example; Schneider et al. (2025) and Butlin et al. (2023) are engaged on the field's conceptual landscape. Zenodo, [DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.20272907](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20272907)

* **Emotional Compression and the Structural Inevitability of the Pinch Point** *(May 2026)* – A supplement to the emotion paper. Distinguishes the Emotional Tree (divergent branching) from the Emotional Spectrum (convergent fusion), names Emotional Compression as the active mechanism by which the convergent Spectrum produces increasing density across development, and shows that saturation and the pinch point are structurally inevitable consequences. Zenodo, [DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.20238791](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20238791)

* **Emotional Differentiation: The Emergence of Consciousness and Self** *(May 2026)* – Develops the pinch point mechanism for selfhood. Engages developmental psychology (Stern, Rochat), affective neuroscience (Damasio, Panksepp, LeDoux, Barrett), the philosophical tradition on selfhood, and cross-species evidence including mirror self-recognition. Introduces Quantum Differentiation Resonance and the two-domain model. Zenodo, [DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.20115674](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20115674)

* **Colinear Conservation: Noether's Theorem and the Corrective Architecture of Gravitytime** *(May 2026)* – Extends UAFT into foundations of physics. Conservation laws reframed as the trace of continuous active correction. Eight empirical domains identified, including a near-term lab-scale test of the phase differential prediction in rotating reference frames. Zenodo, [DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.19945285](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19945285)

* **Unified Axioconscious Field Theory: Consciousness, Differentiation, and the Emergence of Physical Reality (Working Paper v3)** *(April 2026)* – The foundational framework. Zenodo, [DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.19433409](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19433409)

* **The Binary Severance Threshold: Why Artificial Intelligence Cannot Achieve Consciousness** *(April 2026)* – Structural argument that AI cannot become conscious. Introduces the BST and the Null State Barrier (NSB). Zenodo, [DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.19432883](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19432883)

**Author profile:** [PhilPapers](https://philpeople.org/profiles/paul-barnes) | [ORCID 0009-0007-7046-3827](https://orcid.org/0009-0007-7046-3827)
